Data on the 400 zip codes to which Gavin Newsom is giving 40% of the COVID-19 vaccine

California governor Gavin Newsom (@GavinNewsom) is allocating 40% of the state's COVID-19 vaccines to the most "unhealthy" 400 zip codes in the state.

The table below has data derived from map.healthyplacesindex.org and censusreporter.org showing the data on each of those zips. Indexes 0 to 399 are "unhealthy", the rest are "healthy".

Despite what you might expect - or simply because he doesn't think things through - Newsom is allocating 40% of the doses to 60% of the state's population. He's also allocating 10% to the state's teachers (most likely very loosely-defined). Thus, leaving teachers out of it, 60% of the state's doses will go to 40% of the population. Of course, because he's discriminating against people based on where they live (which in many cases corresponds to their race), the 60% in "unhealthy" zip codes can get doses wherever they want, while the 40% in "healthy" zip codes won't be able to get doses meant for those in the special "unhealthy" zip codes. If it all sounds like Jim Crow, that's because it's the suede-denim version of it.
